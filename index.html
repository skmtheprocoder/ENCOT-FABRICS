About Us


With a lineage of 50 years in the cloth business, Encot was established in the year 1998, bringing a fresh and exciting perspective to the ever-evolving textile industry. Our desire to continuously discover something new is the foundation of our growth. Today's fast changing world of textiles and fashion needs constant innovation and new fabric developments to keep pace with the changing trends around the globe. Fashion brands, departmental stores and designers seek inspiration from the wide variety of fabrics and designs we have to offer. Our creative and advanced fabric offerings cover almost every type of design and texture in the market. Our fabrics add a touch of class to executive menswear, women's wear, and kids wear with innovative designs adding to the feel and beauty of our garments. A renowned firm engaged in manufacturing and supplying all kind of fabrics in accordance with preset standards of the industry. Our clients applaud the creativity and durability of our products, together with the exceptional levels of service we provide.
